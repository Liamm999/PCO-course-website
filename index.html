Nature of the unit

Principle of Computing (PCO)

A course of general interest giving experience with typical skills needed for students to pursue specialized programs leading to technical and professional careers and certifications in the IT industry.

Students have an opportunity to investigate career opportunities in six major IT areas: Data and Information, personal computer (PC) hardware, the Binary System and Boolean logic, some typical PC applications such as word-processors and spreadsheets and presentation creating and database management, networking and internet, web page design and website development/maintenance including JavaScript.
